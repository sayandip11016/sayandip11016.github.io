I am a Quantum Physicist with over 5 years of experience in quantum computing, quantum many-body dynamics, quantum error correction, and numerical modeling. Currently, I am a Postdoctoral Researcher at the Virginia Tech Center for Quantum Information Science and Engineering.

I possess strong programming skills (Python, Fortran) and cross-functional expertise in academic, startup, and industry-oriented projects. I hold a proven publication record and have led collaborative projects across quantum information and condensed matter theory.

Prior to Virginia Tech, I held a postdoctoral position at New York University's Center for Quantum Phenomena and worked as a Scientist at Truventic LLC. I received my Ph.D. in Physics from the University of Central Florida under the supervision of Prof. Eduardo Mucciolo, where I studied methods and characterization of topological and disorder-induced protection of coherence in quantum systems.

Please explore my site to learn more about my [publications](/publications/), [experience](/cv/), and research.
